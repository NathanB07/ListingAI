Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ListingAI ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website at listingai.dev and our copy generation service (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: When you sign up, we collect your name and email address.
  • Payment information: Billing details are processed securely by Stripe. We do not store your credit card numbers on our servers.
  • Usage data: We collect information about how you use the Service, including the number of generations you perform, features you use, and time spent in the app.
  • Property data you enter: The property details you enter into our forms are sent to the Anthropic API to generate copy. We do not permanently store the full content of your inputs beyond your session history.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device type, and referring URLs collected automatically via analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Service
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send you transactional emails (receipts, password resets, account notices)
  • To send you our optional marketing newsletter if you opt in (you may unsubscribe at any time)
  • To improve the Service based on usage patterns
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. AI Processing — Third Party Notice

The property details you enter are transmitted to Anthropic, PBC ("Anthropic") via their API to generate copy. By using our Service, you acknowledge that your input data is processed by Anthropic subject to their Privacy Policy. We do not share your personal account information with Anthropic — only the property details you enter in the generation form.

4. Who We Share Your Data With

We share your data only with the following service providers who help us operate:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Anthropic — AI generation
  • Vercel — hosting and infrastructure
  • Google Analytics — anonymous usage analytics

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share it with advertisers.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days upon written request. Generation history stored in your browser session is cleared when you clear your browser data.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, email us at: listingaiservice@gmail.com

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your session. We use analytics cookies (Google Analytics) to understand how people use our site. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work correctly.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure API key storage, and rate limiting on all API endpoints. However, no system is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by posting a notice on our website.
